About Desi Challenge
India's favourite family quiz game, now digital.

Welcome to Desi Challenge – Indian Edition, a free-to-play party trivia game built for Indian families, friends, and anyone who loves desi culture. Whether it's a family get-together, a Diwali night, a road trip, or a rainy Sunday afternoon — Desi Challenge is the game that brings everyone together.

What is Desi Challenge?

Desi Challenge is inspired by the classic Family Feud format, reimagined entirely around Indian culture, food, Bollywood, cricket, festivals, and everyday desi life. Players take turns answering survey-style questions and scoring points by matching the most popular answers. The more popular your answer, the higher the points.

How to Play

  • Add 1 to 8 players on the home screen.
  • Each player takes a turn answering a question.
  • Type your answer — if it matches a survey answer on the board, points are added to your score.
  • 3 wrong answers and the round ends.
  • Rounds increase in difficulty: Easy → Medium → Hard → Expert.
  • The player with the most points at the end wins!

Who Made This?

Desi Challenge was created as a passion project to celebrate Indian culture through interactive play. The game is entirely browser-based, works offline, and requires no app download or account creation. It is completely free to play.
